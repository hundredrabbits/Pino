# Checklist

## Yearly

- Grease Winches
- Flip headsail sheets
- Flip main sheet

## Before any big trip

- Check halyards for chaffing
- Check oil
- Wash sheets
- Check sails for tears

## Bi-yearly

- Check zincs
- Grease prop
- Clean/grease headsail furler

## Monthly

- Run engine 20 minutes
- Water batteries
